We use the MODIS NDVI product and fire product.
The MODIS NDVI product is available at   https://lpdaac.usgs.gov/products/mod13q1v061/ and fire product is available at https://www.earthdata.nasa.gov/data/catalog/lpcloud-mcd64a1-061.
Burn Area and vegtion fire emission simulated by Earth system models is available at CMIP6 data pool (https://aims2.llnl.gov/search/cmip6/)
